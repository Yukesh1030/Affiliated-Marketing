    Common Questions

    What documents do I need for a credit card application?
    Typically, you will need a PAN card, Aadhaar card, proof of income (last 3 months salary slips or ITR), and a residence proof. Some banks may require additional documentation.
    How can I improve my chances of approval?
    Ensure your credit score (CIBIL) is above 750, maintain a stable income source, and avoid applying for multiple cards in a short period.
